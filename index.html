Our rules

"So listen, this ain't a anarchy server, but just follow the basic rules and you'll be fine."

  1. Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, bitch/witch hunting, sexism, racism, or hate speech will not be tolerated. Banter between mates is fine, but don't overdo it.
  2. Keep any arguments to a minimum. This is a chill place for everyone to hangout and enjoy the vibes. If you have an issue with another member, please contact staff.
  3. If you see something against the rules or something that makes you feel unsafe, let staff know. We want this server to be a welcome space for all!
  4. No spam or self-promotion (This includes direct-messaging fellow members) outside of their respective channels and keep convos on topic.
  5. No NSFW or obscene content outside of the "NSFW" channel. This includes text, images, or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence, or other graphically disturbing content.
  6. No blank or zalgo usernames. We will kindly ask that you change it, otherwise we will forcibly change it ourselves.
  7. If you don't like what you see in the server, the door is open for you to leave. This community is a chill place for everyone to hang out, not just for one person.
